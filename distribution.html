Distribution

Mughal Bakery is a brand-friendly co-manufacturer and snack food manufacturer based in Chicago, Illinois. We bring over 35+ years of food experience to every project we produce. We work with small emerging brands as well as large food companies to create manufactured and packaged products ready for retail.

Capabilities

Our core capabilities are:

  • cookie production
  • cracker production
  • puff pastry production
  • breads & bun production
  • sugar-free cookie production
  • packaging consulting
  • and more

We are start-up friendly and do small to large runs. We also do consulting, from idea to on shelf.
