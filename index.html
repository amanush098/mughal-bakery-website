Since 1980

Founders of Mughal Bakery

Founded in the 1980s, Mughal Bakery began small, as a specialized production facility for cake rusk, tea toast and baked goods. In a highly saturated market, there was something special about Mughal that let it thrive where many of its competitors did not.
