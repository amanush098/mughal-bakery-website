We're Thrilled to be Featured on WGN Radio!

Mughal Bakers was recently featured on WGN Radio's “The Bob Sirott Morning Show”! Lieutenant David Haynes, a retired police officer and co-author of “The Beat Cop's Guide to Chicago Eats,” reviewed our bakery, highlighting our variety of cookies, cream puffs, and hand pies (see the segment here).

We're incredibly grateful to Hayley Boyd and Lieutenant Haynes for featuring Mughal Bakers on their show. It's an honor to be recognized alongside other amazing Chicago eateries.

A Special Thanks to Our Customers

This recognition wouldn't be possible without the continued support of our loyal customers. Thank you for choosing Mughal Bakers and for helping us share our passion for delicious baked goods with the Chicago community!

Stop by and Try Our Featured Treats!

If you haven't already, come visit us and try the cookies, cream puffs, and hand pies mentioned on the radio show. We also have a wide selection of other delectable pastries, cakes, and breads. We look forward to seeing you soon!
